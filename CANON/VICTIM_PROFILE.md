# Victim Profile

## Basic Information

**Full Name:** Brittany "Britt" Johansson
**Nickname/Handle:** @CleanEatsBritt
**Age:** 27
**Nationality:** American (Minnesota)
**Occupation:** Fitness influencer / Online personal trainer

## Archetype
**Primary:** The Wellness Warrior

**Secondary traits:** Type-A perfectionist, calorie counter, unwitting hypocrite

## The Fatal Flaw
**Core Flaw:** Selective blindness

**How it manifests:**
- Refuses to eat street food but trusts hostel buffets
- Brings protein powder but not antacids
- Lectures others on health while making dangerous choices
- Believes her "strong immune system" makes her invincible

**How it kills them:**
Her obsessive focus on macros blinds her to actual food safety. She chooses the "healthier" option (eggs over pastries) not realizing the eggs have been sitting at room temperature for hours.

## Physical Appearance
- **Height/Build:** 5'7", lean athletic build, visible muscle definition
- **Hair:** Blonde, always in a high ponytail or braids
- **Distinguishing features:** Perfect white teeth, spray tan lines, motivational tattoo ("STRONGER" on inner forearm)
- **Typical outfit:** Matching activewear sets, sports bra visible, Apple Watch, resistance bands in bag

## Personality

### Positive Traits (that get twisted)
1. Disciplined - becomes rigid and judgmental about food
2. Encouraging to others - becomes condescending about health choices

### Negative Traits (that seal their fate)
1. Arrogance about her body's capabilities - leads to ignoring warning signs
2. Performative wellness - leads to eating for content rather than safety

### Pet Peeves
- People who don't track their food
- Artificial ingredients in anything

### Fears
- Surface fear: Gaining weight while traveling
- Deeper fear: Being ordinary, losing relevance

## Background

### Origin Story
Grew up in suburban Minneapolis in a family that struggled with obesity and diabetes. Lost 60 pounds in college and made her transformation her entire identity. Built a following of 340,000 on Instagram. Never recovered from being the fat kid; every meal is a battle against her past self.

### Why They Travel
"Wellness retreat content" - traveling through Southeast Asia visiting yoga retreats and healthy cafes for sponsored content. Also escaping a recent breakup she hasn't told her followers about.

### How They Ended Up at This Hostel
The yoga retreat fell through last minute (scam). Booked the cheapest available option to save money for content-worthy experiences. "It's just one night."

## Voice & Speech

### Vocabulary
- Uses words like: "macro-friendly," "gains," "fuel your body," "clean eating," "mindful"
- Avoids words like: "diet" (it's a "lifestyle"), "fat" (it's "fluffy")

### Internal Monologue Style
Constantly calculating: calories in, calories out, steps taken, protein consumed. Narrates her life as Instagram captions.
